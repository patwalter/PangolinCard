Pangolin

The word 'pangolin' comes from the Malay word 'penggulung', which means 'one that rolls up'.

      Scientific Name: Pholidota
      Average Legnth: 12-39 in
      Lifespan: 20 years
      Habitat: Dense forest to forested savannas

The pangolin is a little known creature found in Africa and pat of Asia. Though the pangolin have sharp claws. Thier main line of defense is rolling up into a ball and protecting itself from predeators. Thier scales are made of keratin, the same structure that makes up human nails.
